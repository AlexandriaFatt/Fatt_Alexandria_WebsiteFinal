Blending Flavors and Cultural Heritage

Welcome to Kasama, where we blend the diverse tastes and traditions of the Philippines. Our menu reflects the rich tapestry of Filipino cuisine inspired by our culinary heritage. Join us for a dining experience that unites the soulful flavors of tradition with a modern twist.

Our Story

Kasama, situated in the East Ukrainian Village neighborhood of Chicago, is a bakery and contemporary Filipino restaurant co-founded by chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores. We eagerly anticipate the pleasure of hosting you!
